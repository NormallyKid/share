instead of using file:// as our reference, we will use PHP's php://filter/ wrapper. With this filter, we can specify the convert.base64-encode encoder as our filter, and then add an input resource (e.g. resource=index.php), as follows:
<!DOCTYPE email [
  <!ENTITY company SYSTEM "php://filter/convert.base64-encode/resource=index.php">
]>
